Our History

Since 1999, VHotel has stood as a legendary hotel in Jakarta, renowned for its impeccable service. With a rich history spanning over two decades, VHotel has continuously provided a haven of luxury and comfort for its guests. From its humble beginnings, the hotel has evolved into an iconic destination, offering an assortment of rooms, from cozy single-bedrooms to lavish three-bedroom suites with kitchens. With top-notch facilities, including spas, gyms, and pools, VHotel has remained committed to exceeding guests' expectations and creating memorable experiences.

Explore Us

At VhoTel, we understand that every guest is unique, which is why we offer an assortment of rooms to cater to diverse preferences. From our comfortable and cozy 1-bed rooms to our spacious 2-bed rooms, and our opulent 3-bedroom suites with a fully equipped kitchen, we provide a range of accommodation options to suit every need.
Beyond our luxurious rooms, VhoTel is dedicated to offering an exceptional experience through our world-class facilities. Indulge in ultimate relaxation at our rejuvenating spa, where expert therapists provide a range of treatments to pamper and revitalize your senses. Stay active and maintain your fitness routine in our state-of-the-art gym, equipped with the latest exercise equipment. Take a refreshing dip in our inviting pools, surrounded by lush greenery, and soak up the sun in a tranquil oasis within the city.
What truly sets VhoTel apart is our unwavering commitment to delivering personalized service. Our dedicated team of staff, renowned for their warm hospitality and attention to detail, are always ready to go the extra mile to ensure that your stay is nothing short of extraordinary. Whether it's arranging bespoke city tours, recommending local attractions, or curating unique dining experiences, we strive to exceed your expectations and create unforgettable memories.
Come and experience the legacy of VhoTel, where exceptional service, luxurious accommodations, and world-class facilities converge to create an unparalleled stay in Jakarta. We invite you to embark on a journey of indulgence and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
